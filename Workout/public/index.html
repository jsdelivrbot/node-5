Fitted: Your Work Out App


Powerful Tips for Beginners



Motivation

Why do we work out? What is your goal? People work out for many reasons, whether to look good, get fit, stay or get healthy, or to be able to lift more weight, etc. There could be many. Define your reason and define your goals.

Plan

Choose your work outs wisely. First, get to know what work outs are out there and make plans: categorize your work out, whether it would be cardio, strength, resistance, or by body parts such as arms, legs, or shoulders. Make it simple and write down your plans to carry them out.

Exercise

Once you have categorized and planned your work outs, make sure you pull through. Make yourself accountable. It's easy to skip out on a work out if you are sore one day. That is the whole point of categorizing and working out different parts of your bodies each day. Boost up your motivation and create a playlist for your workout: music always helps.
